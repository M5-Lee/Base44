# Trading Intelligence Ecosystem

This repository is part of a multi-project trading intelligence ecosystem designed to support traders across three major layers.

## Layer 1 — Data Intelligence

The data intelligence layer focuses on collecting and structuring raw market information.

Responsibilities include:

- earnings calendar ingestion
- options chain enrichment
- open interest analysis
- expected move calculations
- max pain analysis

Primary system:

Earning Edge

This layer transforms raw market data into structured signals.

## Layer 2 — Market Intelligence

The market intelligence layer aggregates real-time macro and futures information.

Responsibilities include:

- ES futures monitoring
- macro news aggregation
- geopolitical event tracking
- tariff news monitoring
- economic calendar analysis

Primary system:

ES Trader Intel

This layer helps traders quickly understand the current macro and market environment.

## Layer 3 — Trader Development

The trader development layer focuses on improving trader skill and decision making.

Responsibilities include:

- trading education
- pattern recognition training
- execution simulations
- chart analysis exercises

Primary system:

Execution Lab

This layer focuses on building trader capability rather than delivering market signals.

## System philosophy

The ecosystem is designed around a simple idea.

Traders perform best when they combine:

1. structured market data
2. real-time macro awareness
3. disciplined execution skills

Each project focuses on one of these components.

## Long-term direction

Over time these systems may integrate more tightly.

Possible integration directions include:

- shared signal pipelines
- cross-platform analytics
- unified trader dashboards

This repository contributes to the broader trading intelligence platform.
