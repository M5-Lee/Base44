# Vision

## Why this platform exists

Earning Edge exists to turn raw earnings and options data into structured decision support for traders.

Most retail traders see headlines, scattered option chains, and isolated metrics. Earning Edge is designed to compress that noise into a cleaner signal layer.

## Core mission

Help traders identify:

- which earnings names matter
- how options are positioned around those names
- where bullish or bearish pressure is concentrated
- when the setup is strong enough to deserve attention

## Role in the broader platform

Earning Edge is the **data intelligence layer** of the broader trading ecosystem.

It focuses on:

- earnings event discovery
- options sentiment and positioning
- structured signal generation

## Long-term direction

Over time, Earning Edge should evolve toward:

- cleaner signal normalization
- stronger backtesting and validation
- repeatable signal quality scoring
- more reliable decision support for options traders

## Guiding principle

The goal is not to overwhelm traders with more data.

The goal is to turn complex options positioning into clearer judgment.
